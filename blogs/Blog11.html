Reading: The Road to Technocolonialism.

The reading focuses on the effects AI could have on the world, introducing a new form of colonialism called 'Technocolonialism'. This could pose a threat to underdevelop regions, especially from more technologically advanced regions. The more developed countried might lend newer technology to less developed countries, in exchange for political gain. This could also open up the possibility of more increased surveillance.
