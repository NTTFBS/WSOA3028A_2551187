The Internet and Ethics.

The internet, by itself, is not a problem. It has been used for both good and bad, in the case of bridging the communication gap between people.


However, the internet has the ability to add another layer of security, or more appropriately, anonymity, to those who use it.

I believe that this is where the ethics and morals of individuals are on full display. The anonymity acts as a mask, allowing users to act without any immediately perceivable consequences. Those who use it for wrong find it to be a space where they can hide, which can be dangerous to society.
